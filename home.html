Meet Our Trainers-

Cardio Gym Trainer

Duties: A cardio gym trainer holds certification from organizations like ACE, NASM, or ACSM and has experience creating personalized cardio workout plans. They possess knowledge of cardiovascular physiology and exercise science, ensuring safe and effective training. Proficiency in using and instructing on cardio equipment, combined with strong motivational skills, helps keep clients committed. They also conduct fitness assessments and monitor progress to adjust workouts for optimal results.

Gym Trainer

Duties: A seasoned gym trainer with expertise in crafting personalized fitness plans tailored to individual goals and needs. With certification from reputable fitness organizations, they excel in guiding clients through effective exercise techniques and equipment usage. Their passion for fitness is matched by their dedication to motivating and supporting clients on their journey to achieving their health and wellness goals. As a knowledgeable and approachable trainer, is committed to fostering a positive and empowering gym environment for all.

Power Lifter

Duties: A dedicated powerlifter specializing in squat, bench press, and deadlift. With a focus on strength and technique, they follow structured training programs and prioritize nutrition and recovery for optimal performance. Their mental resilience and determination drive them to push their limits in training and competitions. embodies the spirit of powerlifting, continually striving for personal bests and fostering a supportive community within the sport.
